# About Me

I am a backend engineer who focuses on building reliable, maintainable API systems. I work with Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and modern backend tooling to create systems that are predictable, stable, and easy to extend.

## What I Do
I design and build backend architectures that emphasize clarity, correctness, and long term maintainability. My work includes:

- API design and implementation
- Authentication and authorization systems
- Role and permission models
- Background job pipelines
- Database modeling and migrations
- Automated testing
- CI and deployment workflows

## How I Work
I focus on clean architecture, strong data modeling, and predictable behavior. I prefer simple, readable solutions over clever ones. I build systems that are easy to understand, easy to test, and easy to maintain.
